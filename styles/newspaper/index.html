About This Design

Newspaper web designs have a distinct look that sets them apart from blogs, social media, and other informational websites. They face a tremendous challenge in showing huge amounts of information in limited screen sizes. Often, it can be overwhelming to peruse as the website balances the need to show all the highlights, links, and ads at one time.
